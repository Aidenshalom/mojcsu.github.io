Support Our Mission

Partner with Us to Create Lasting Impact

At the Community Service Unit (CSU), we are committed to transforming lives through volunteerism, social development, and rehabilitation programs. By partnering with us, your organization can play a crucial role in creating positive change in communities across Lagos State.

Ways You Can Support Us

✅ Corporate Sponsorship - Support our initiatives through financial contributions or resources.
✅ Workplace Volunteering - Encourage your employees to give back through structured community service activities.
✅ In-Kind Donations - Provide essential items such as food, educational materials, healthcare supplies, and sanitation equipment.
✅ Collaborative Projects - Work with us on specific programs that align with your company's CSR goals.

Why Partner with Us?

  • Visible Social Impact - Your support directly improves lives and strengthens communities.
  • Brand Recognition - We highlight our partners on our platforms and events.
  • Networking & Collaboration - Join a network of like-minded organizations driving social change.
