Partner with Us to Create Lasting Impact

At the Lagos State Community Service Unit (CSU), we are committed to fostering restorative justice by implementing non-custodial sentences for minor offenders. This approach not only alleviates prison congestion but also enables offenders to contribute positively to society through supervised community service tasks.

Why Partner with Us?

Restorative Justice in Action: Your support enables us to offer court-mandated community service as a constructive alternative to traditional incarceration. By providing meaningful assignments, we help offenders rebuild their lives and contribute positively to society.
Corporate Social Responsibility with Purpose: Align your company with a transformative initiative that not only supports community development but also enhances public safety. Partnering with us demonstrates your commitment to restorative justice and social innovation.
Innovative Impact: Our program leverages structured community service as a tool for reducing recidivism and fostering personal growth among participants. Your partnership empowers us to create and expand these impactful programs.
