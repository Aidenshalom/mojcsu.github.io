What We Offer

Facilitate Employment Opportunities

Collaborate with local businesses and vocational training centers to secure job placements and apprenticeships for rehabilitated offenders.

Partner with Social Services and N.G.Os

Work with social welfare agencies to provide housing, healthcare, and other essential services that support successful reintegration.

Court-Mandated Service Support

We assist with court-mandated community service, offering easy registration, assignment guidance, and support to ensure you complete your obligations smoothly.

Overseeing Non-Custodial Sentences

The Community Service Unit in Lagos manages non-custodial sentences, ensuring minor offenders contribute to society through activities like street cleaning and community farming. This promotes rehabilitation, reduces prison congestion, and encourages responsibility.

Community Development Programs

Leads neighborhood improvement projects such as environmental clean-ups, tree planting, and infrastructure repairs.

Education and Awareness Campaigns

Offers training on health, sanitation, personal development, and civic responsibility.

Assigning Meaningful Tasks

Providing Supervision Across Lagos

With 18 locations throughout Lagos, the unit closely supervises offenders to ensure community service is completed effectively. This oversight helps offenders learn from their experiences while fulfilling their obligations.

Enhancing Community Well-Being

By involving offenders in tasks like sanitation and aiding nursing homes, the unit improves public spaces and fosters a stronger community. These efforts benefit residents and promote a sense of shared responsibility.

Supporting Rehabilitation

The unit offers offenders a chance to reflect and contribute to society through service. This rehabilitative approach aids reintegration, lowers reoffending rates, and helps individuals become productive community members.
